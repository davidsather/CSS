Wireless Rig

Dom's MPR Wireless Rig (proposed)

System Purpose

The purpose of this syetem is to allow the MPR worship leader to enter and exit the stage during a service, (which is often a time sensitive situation) with greater ease. An added benefit is the ability to walk the room during sound check allowing for their input in the overall sound in the room.

System Overview

The system consists of Shure UR1 body pack transmitters paired with the UR4D reciever for both the electric and accoustic guitars to be used wireless. We would also use the PSM 900 wireless IEM along with a case by SKB and a Furman power supply to power everything and keep things neat. The SKB Case could also serve as a place for Dom to put his laptop on stage and power it.
